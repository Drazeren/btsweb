BT21

BTS worked with Webtoon to make their cartoon characters, BT21, into a sticker collection for their app LINE. It's basically like emojis for the app when you message your friends using the app. There are seven characters that was drawn individually by the seven members of BTS to represent them.

TATA

Tata, drawn by V, is an alien from BT planet. He was lonely and wanted to travel around finding friends. Naturally during his travels to different planet, he gained a curious personality. Tata can also stretch its limbs at any lengths, and transform himself into different objects. When V drew Tata, he wanted him to have a unique look rather than just look really cute.

RJ

RJ, drawn by Jin, is an alpaca that enjoys eating (much like his creator Jin). He is kind and polite, and the cutest while mumbling. RJ wears a parka he got from Machu Piccu and wears it because he gets cold easily. RJ as well is from Machu Piccu. Jin had been working on the drawings for RJ for months and really wanted to go for a cute looking character.

CHIMMY

Chimmy, drawn by Jimin, wears a yellow hoodie, and always has his tongue out. Chimmy was born with passion and tries hard at everything, being nicknamed the king of passion. But he is also considered a charming narcissit. Its believed Jimin got his idea for the look from when Jimin himself had to wear a dog onesie and pretend to be a dog for their small skit: House of Army.

COOKY

Cooky, drawn by Jungkook, is a pink bunny, with a unique eyebrow, whose goal is to be strong. His workout routine consists of doing two hours on the "running machine" 400 push-ups, and 100 minutes of eyebrow drawing. Cooky is rough to everyone, but even rougher to his friends. When he's angry, he changes from a small bunny, to a big muscular bunny. Jungkook is already a good artist, so when he first drew Cooky, he made him look to realistic, like a person's face with bunny ears. Then he drew the bunny to be tall with abs and strong arms, which RM joked saying you drew a self portrait, as Jungkook's body is similar to what he originally drew.

SHOOKY

Shooky, drawn by Suga, is the smallest character. Although he looks like a cookie and his enenmy is milk (hates milk a lot) he says he is a really delicious shooky, not a cookie. He usually naps with Koya and although he's tiny, he can be loud and make a mess. Shooky often argues with Cooky.

MANG

Mang, drawn by J-Hope, is nicknamed the dancing king/ dancing machine, just how J-Hope loves to dance and is the main dancer. He is always break dancing and is sweet. He wears a horse mask always. Thus, no one knows his true face. With his friends, he's always happy, making jokes, and acts cool. But underneath it all, he hides his true self and is the sensitive one.

KOYA

Koya, drawn by RM, has droopy eyes, and a docile apperance. But, Koya is a koala that has a surprisingly delicate and sharp judgement, plus musical skills. He sometimes looks like he's sleeping, but is actually deeply thinking. Also he is actually suffering from insomnia. Many moments, Koya thinks a lot but when he thinks about his big ears falling off, he gets startled.

VAN

Van represents ARMY! It is a giant space robot that protects BT21. Van is a reprsentation of ARMY because half of the robot is white and grey, symbolically representing day and night and how ARMYs live around the world and live in differnt time zones. The X and O, being the eyes of Van, is like hugs and kisses, as BTS loves ARMY.
