RM (Kim Namjoon)

RM, once Rap Monster, is the leader of the group and one of the rappers of the group.He was the first one recruited for the group and only one who can speak English fluently, thus he is usually the one who does the talking in English interviews. When BTS is writing their own songs, usually the producers take RM's writing into the songs. His IQ is said to be on the genius level. Although he is the leader, he is not the oldest member. He was born on September 12, 1994, has a dog name Rapmon, and is the clumsiest member of thr group. He has been seen in videos breaking glasses he just bought, beds, the glass floor from dancing, and even simply standing there but the bag he was carrying ripped. RM used to be called Rap Monster, but he felt as he got older he wanted a more professional name so he initialized it, into RM. RM also has his own songs such as Change with rapper Wale. RM is highly respected by his other members and although may have a strong apperance, he is very sweet.

Suga (Min Yoongi)

Suga is another rapper in the group and got his name because when he smiles he looks sweet like sugar. Suga is from Daegu was born on March 9th, 1993, but is jokingly called the child of the group by members, but grandpa by ARMYs. He is very passionate about music. Before debut he started music in 6th grade. In middle school he learned classical composition and started writng songs daily after that. Before BTS, he was very poor and it also affected his mental health, He gravely suffered depression as he speaks about in his mixtape Agust D, song Last of Me. He dreamt of going far with music that he would have to choose between either spening money on food or transportation to take him to places that will help him continue his music. This has caused him to go nights and days without eating. Now he spends hours in his own little studio, working on music and song writing. Suga although in pictures he looks sweet, he is also considered the "savage" of the group, making sarcastic remarks and making faces when members act silly or extra. He has a dog named Holy, loves to play the piano, and is really good at playing basketball, and loves to sleep.

J-Hope (Jung Ho Seok)

J-HOOOPPPPEEEE! He is also the rapper of the group and the main dancer. He can also sing and sometimes takes part as a vocalist in the group. Jhope was born on February 18, 1994 in Gwangju. In the beginning, RM says he has never seen a kid more depressed than J-Hope. Now he is nicknamed the Sunshine of the group, as his smile and laugh is so contagious, it warms our hearts. He is often extremely cheerful and most energetic of the group. J-Hope had been dancing before BTS and used to be backup dancer for others. His father did not approve of him wanting to dance and to look into something more practical, but his mother continued to support him, thus the reason he became the man he is today (which is discussed in his solo songs in Wings, Mama). Pre-debut J-Hope was a part of a street dance team, called NEURON. He won an underground dance battle and even performed at a festival. J-Hope used to be a tennis player in elementary school and played in competitions. He once received a bronze medal, out of 3 team competitors. He also has an older sister, Jiwoo, who is very active on instagram and helps take care of their dog, Mickey. He is soon going to have out his own mixtape.

Jimin (Park Jimin)

Jimin was born on October 13, 1995. He is nicknamed Chim Chim and mochi as he had chubby cheeks when they first debuted, and it reminded fans of the Japanese rice cake. He was recruited from his local province of Busan as part of Big Hit's "Hit It" auditions in 2010 and 2011 to be a part of Bangtan Boys. Jimin is also one of the vocalists and main dancers as he has been dancing years prior to BTS. He used to dance to contempary music and then went to Hip Hop. Jimin is often usually the one to sing the high notes and posts the most on their twitter page. He has a very sweet and shy personality, but also has a very flirty side to him as well. It was revealed this year after their comeback in September 2017, that he had almost been kicked out of the group before their debut date (June 13, 2013) several times, even once the day before the debut. The reason being that people assumed he would not make it or keep up with the group, or be popular with the fans. He also over the years has been struggling with body image issues and self esteem. Jimin put himself stricter diets and would often feel light headed and faint when dancing for music videos or doing dance practice. He now has assured fans he will no longer be doing these diets and will stay healthy and eat well.

Jungkook is also compared to a bunny because of his small face and his teeth comes out when he smiles reminds ARMYs of teeth like a bunny. He is the main vocalist, few times does the rap, often in the center when dancing or in the front, may not look it but is very muscular. He has an older brother, and a dog named Gureum. He enjoys playing video games, reading manga, watching anime, and his own wardrobe consists of mainly white t-shirts. Jungkook is very shy around girls and often teases the other members. When debuted, he was just 15 years old. Favourite foods are bread, pizza, and anything with flour, pork soup rice, sashimi, sea eel. His role model is G- Dragon, another Korean idol from once boy group Big Bang. He thanks the other members for helping him grow into the man he is today.
